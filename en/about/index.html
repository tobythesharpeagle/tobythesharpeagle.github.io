Toby, co-founder

Born on October 16, 2020, Toby is a 30-lb, Shar Pei-Beagle (Sharpeagle) mix with a nose for adventure. His passion for exploration often leads him to uncover hidden scents and sprint across open fields with boundless joy. Don’t be fooled by his innocent gaze, though. Toby has a knack for transforming dolls and toys into delightful piles of fluff. When the sun graces the sky, you’ll find him basking in its warm glow, lazily indulging in afternoon naps. As for his culinary preferences, Toby can be quite the connoisseur. His taste buds tingle with delight at the mere mention of chicken, making it his all-time favorite snack or mealtime treat. Squirrels and rabbits, beware! Toby’s relentless chase is legendary, and no amount of training can deter his pursuit of these little furry friends. Beneath his mischievous nature lies a heart of gold, as he showers everyone he meets with sweetness and adoration.


Phoebe, co-founder

Meet Phoebe, the passionate co-founder of TobyTime and the beloved owner of our furry protagonist, Toby. With a tech-savvy mind and a heart full of love for animals, Phoebe brings a unique blend of skills and affection to our canine-centric world. When she’s not immersed in the exciting realm of technology, Phoebe’s true joy lies in spoiling Toby rotten. From gourmet treats to stylish accessories, she showers him with the finest indulgences. But Phoebe’s love for Toby doesn’t stop at material delights. She yearns to create unforgettable memories and show Toby the wonders of the world. During her free time, she becomes an adventure architect, meticulously planning their travels and explorations to new and exciting destinations.


Join us at TobyTime, as we embark on remarkable adventures and playful escapades!
