Embark on an adventure and explore the scenic dog-friendly walks near West Hollywood, including Griffith Park, Franklin Canyon, and UCLA Botanical Garden.

Join Toby and me as we explore the wonders of Los Angeles, venturing within a 10-mile radius of West Hollywood, to discover our top three favorite spots.

Picnic and Pictures: Griffith Park

Griffith Park, nestled in the heart of Los Angeles, is an iconic park with a network of trails that are just perfect for leisurely strolls and exploration alongside your furry companion. As you wander along the park’s trails, prepare to be captivated by breathtaking vistas, including the world-famous Hollywood Sign and the Griffith Observatory.

Now here’s the cherry on top: there’s no entrance fee to experience the wonders of Griffith Park! So gather your loved ones, leash up your furry friends, and embark on an unforgettable adventure, where nature’s beauty is sure to put a smile on your face at every turn.

For more details about our memorable experience at Griffith Park, including helpful parking and trail information, check out this blog post!

Coffee and Stroll: UCLA Botanical Garden

Not only is the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) renowned for its exceptional academic programs, but it also boasts a stunning campus in the Westwood neighborhood. Prepare for a delightful adventure as you explore not only the charming Westwood Village with its shops and coffee spots, but also the hidden gem of the free UCLA Botanical Garden.

Step into the botanical garden, a small yet enchanting paradise that will transport you to the heart of a whimsical forest. As you meander through the garden’s pathways, your furry friend will be in heaven, exploring a wonderland of captivating scents. The garden also offers plenty of benches where you can relax, perhaps delving into a book while your furry companion basks in the sun or takes a nice nap.

If you’re curious to hear about our afternoon spent in Westwood Village and the UCLA Botanical Garden, don’t miss out on this blog post!

Quiet and Challenging: Franklin Canyon

Franklin Canyon is an expansive park within the beautiful Beverly Hills landscape. With its abundance of picnic areas and scenic trails, this park offers a tranquil escape from the bustling city, minus the crowds you might encounter in other parks like the Griffith Park. It’s a haven for hiking enthusiasts, catering to those with a moderate level of fitness and a love for outdoor exploration.

In the springtime, Franklin Canyon comes alive with a vibrant display of wildflowers, especially after the rare California rain showers. You and your furry friend will certainly be enchanted by the blossoming colors as you traverse the trails, all while enjoying breathtaking views of the iconic Beverly Hills surroundings.

Check out this blog post where we recount our exhilarating hiking experience on the Franklin Canyon Hastain Trail, the southernmost trail within this vast park. Let our adventure inspire your own exploration of this remarkable destination!
